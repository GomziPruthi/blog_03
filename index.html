What measures could make the use of AI more transparent and explainable to the public?

https://www.port.ac.uk/news-events-and-blogs/blogs/future-and-emerging-technologies/what-measures-could-make-the-use-of-ai-more-transparent-and-explainable-to-the-public September 5,2023

AI's opacity can be a problem, but it can also be a solution. Inherently transparent AI technologies can illuminate opaque ones. Machine learning, a powerful tool, is often used for classification tasks but has opaque mechanisms. To address transparency and explainability, we should move away from using a single-technology version of AI and instead use a full AI toolkit that includes concept-rich knowledge-based systems and data-driven algorithms. This approach can help progress towards the original definition of AI while addressing issues of explainability and transparency.
